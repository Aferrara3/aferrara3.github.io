Retained Talent

Smarter Hiring Starts with Smarter Predictions Hiring the right people isn’t just about skills and culture fit—it’s about longevity. Employee turnover costs companies millions in lost productivity, recruitment expenses, and onboarding efforts. Retained Talent uses data-driven insights to help companies make hiring decisions with confidence by predicting a candidate’s estimated tenure before they even start.

Our innovative approach evaluates key hiring data to generate a confidence score on how long an employee is likely to stay under current conditions. By providing real-time insights before an offer is made, companies can optimize their hiring strategies, reduce attrition, and build teams that last.
