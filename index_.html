Haplit

In the fall of 2015 I joined Team Haplit in working to create a more affordable learning aid for the visually impaired through Georgia Tech's Idea 2 Prototype (I2P) Program and CreateX Startup Summer. Haplit is a peripheral device designed to produce refreshable braille writing based on the input provided from a connected bluetooth device such as your smartphone. We are still in devlopment stages, however we have produced a working 10x scale version of our unique actuation design, and are currently involved with early efforts to manufacture at scale. Our team has been making great progress, and Haplit was awarded "Best Overall Project" this past fall semester in the entire I2P program.
